# PP-Pack Landing Page Design Philosophy

## Reference Design
This is a **replication task** — the design mockups provided are the ground-truth specification. Fidelity to the reference overrides all other design guidance.

## Design Approach: Modern Minimalist B2B

**Design Movement:** Contemporary B2B Industrial Design with Clean Brutalism
- Emphasis on clarity, functionality, and direct communication
- Strong typography hierarchy with bold sans-serif headlines
- Strategic use of green accent color (brand signature)
- Generous whitespace and asymmetric layouts
- Product-centric photography with clean backgrounds

**Core Principles:**
1. **Clarity First** — Every element serves a purpose; no decorative excess
2. **Product Hero** — The bottle is the visual centerpiece; layout emphasizes it
3. **Trust Through Transparency** — Form fields, icons, and information architecture build confidence
4. **Functional Beauty** — Design supports conversion without sacrificing aesthetics

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Primary Green** (#2D8659 or similar) — Brand signature, buttons, accents, icons
- **Dark Navy/Charcoal** (#1A1F2E) — Headlines, strong text
- **Light Neutral** (off-white, #F8F9FA) — Backgrounds, breathing room
- **Muted Gray** (#6B7280) — Body text, secondary information
- **Accent Highlights** — Soft green backgrounds for form sections

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric hero with bottle on right, content on left
- Product-centric sections with image-text balance
- Card-based grid for benefits (2-3 columns)
- Full-width form section with subtle background
- Footer with contact info and legal links

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Green Icon System** — Outlined icons (microwave, shield, drop, bottle, etc.) in brand green
2. **Bold Typography** — Large, confident headlines with green accent words
3. **Bottle Photography** — Clean, professional product images on light backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Buttons are primary action drivers (green, rounded, with arrow icons)
- Form fields have clear labels with icons
- Hover states provide subtle feedback (slight color shift, shadow)
- CTAs are prominent and action-oriented

**Animation:**
- Smooth scroll transitions (fade-in on scroll)
- Button hover: slight scale and shadow increase
- Form focus: subtle green underline expansion
- No excessive motion; functional and purposeful

**Typography System:**
- **Headlines:** Bold sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat, Roboto Bold, or system bold)
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto Regular)
- **Hierarchy:** Large (32-48px) for section titles, medium (18-24px) for subsections, regular (14-16px) for body

**Brand Essence:**
*Reliable, professional packaging solutions for food producers who demand safety and convenience.*
- **Personality:** Trustworthy, innovative, practical, professional

**Brand Voice:**
- Headlines are direct and benefit-focused: "PP-бутылки для горячего розлива" (not "Welcome to our site")
- CTAs are action-oriented: "Получить образцы" (Get Samples), "Запросить КП" (Request Quote)
- Microcopy is clear and reassuring: "Оставьте заявку — мы свяжемся с вами в течение 1 рабочего дня"

**Logo & Branding:**
- Hexagonal badge with "PP" initials in green
- Clean, geometric, professional
- Used in header and footer

**Signature Brand Color:**
- **PP-Pack Green:** #2D8659 (or similar forest green) — unmistakably the brand's own

## Implementation Notes

- **Hero Section:** Asymmetric layout with text on left, bottle image on right, product use-cases on far right
- **Benefits:** Icon + text cards in grid layout
- **Form Section:** Light background, organized into logical field groups
- **Contact Footer:** Three-column layout (contacts, hours, quick link)
- **Footer:** Dark background with logo, copyright, and legal links

All sections follow the reference design mockups precisely.
